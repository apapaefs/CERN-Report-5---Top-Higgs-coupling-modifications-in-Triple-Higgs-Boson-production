The analysis of the propagation of the renormalization/factorization scale uncertainties combined with the top-mass scheme and scale uncertainties arrives at a linear combination with the uncertainties of the NNLO$_{\mbox{FTapprox}}$ uncertainties based on arguments of the soft and collinear structure of higher-order QCD corrections \cite{Baglio:2020wgt}. The resulting recommended uncertainties read
\begin{eqnarray}
\sqrt{s} = 13~{\rm TeV}: \quad 
\sigma_{tot} & = & 31.05^{+6\%}_{-23\%}~{\rm fb} \\
\sqrt{s} = 14~{\rm TeV}: \quad
\sigma_{tot} & = & 36.69^{+6\%}_{-23\%}~{\rm fb} \\
\sqrt{s} = 27~{\rm TeV}: \quad
\sigma_{tot} & = & 139.9^{+5\%}_{-22\%}~{\rm fb} \\
\sqrt{s} = 100~{\rm TeV}: \quad 
\sigma_{tot} & = & 1224^{+4\%}_{-21\%}~{\rm fb}
\end{eqnarray}
It needs to be noted that these uncertainties do not include uncertainties related to electroweak corrections.
